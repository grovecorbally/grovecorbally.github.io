The Grove Gastro Pub

About us:

Limerick city's newest Gastropub serving top quality locally sourced food. Our building consists of a spacious downstairs with large room upstairs for functions and events. We cater to large and small groups alike with a wide variaty drinks and cocktails. Food is served daily with carvery every sunday

Some of our services:

  • Parties and functions (Large area upstairs)
  • Bookings available, but not essiential
  • Limited Parking outside
  • Table Service
  • Lunch and Dinner
  • Carvery on Sundays

We are located in Corbally road in Limerick city, please see the map for specific directions and address.
